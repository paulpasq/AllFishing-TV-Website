About Me

AllFishingTV started as an idea. I fell in love with fishing around senior year of high school and have been in love with it ever since. My love for video making came soon after. First I started shooting videos on whatever I had, my phone, and old camcorder and much more. A lot changed when I bought my first GoPro. I used that for every video and it offered the best quality money could buy at the time. Then I bought a new professional grade camera and took the videos to the next level. I started posting more and more and before you know it I had gained a significant following. I continue to fish as much as I can but sometimes life gets in the way.


AllFishingTV follows everything from my everyday fishing to the weekend fishing trips to some of the largest lakes in the Northeast. My minnow trap series brought massive amounts of people to my channel and helped me gain a lot of recognition. I used the fish I caught in the minnow traps to stock a pond I made in my backyard. I usually post a pond update once or twice a month but I am constantly building and sculpting it into a better home for my fish. I also upload many fishing gear hauls, fishing contests, fishing challenges, vlogs, and much more. I typically fish for large mouth bass but occasionally I fish for small mouth bass, carp, trout, minnow trapping, stripped bass, charter fishing, and much more. The channel is my way of entertaining the fans while making sure I never stop doing one of the things I love. The channel has been growing rapidly and several milestones have been hit. I held a massive giveaway at 10,000 subscribers and will be holding another one at 20,000. As for future content, I will continue to fish and make the best videos I can. There are some weeks however I cannot make a video because life gets in the way but I will be sure to post any updates on the blog page which can be found here.
